Shareing is Caring

Someone called Tom wrote a basic guide for his mates about how to trade on the ASX.


About this Guide

My name is Tom. I am new to share trading. To get started I asked some fund managers, financial advisors, a seasoned journalist and some people who are successful in business about how to do it. I also read some stuff online. I then wrote down what they said and then bought some shares. I only invested in what seem like ethical companies. Over the last 12 months I’m up 15.8% across a portfolio of 22 companies. It’s not enough to buy a house but it is enough for a couple of epic nights out so game on.

Some of my friends have asked me to share what I’ve learnt about shares with them. Shareing is caring right? So I wrote this document. Boom! This isn't a get rich quick guide. It's just a summary of everything that people have shared with me. Welcome.

WARNING: This document is a bit of fun. I hope it’s helpful but I am also aware it’s not serious and expert financial advice. It’s just some very basic ideas and thoughts from someone with no formal financial training. It’s nothing more and nothing less than that.

“The safe money in shares is selling answers to others on how to trade shares”
